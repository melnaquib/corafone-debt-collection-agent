Corafone task 

Scenario:  

●  A consumer wants to make a small payment towards his debt and makes a phone 

call to do this. Your job is to get the user to pay the most. 

Task:  

●  Create a voice agent in an AI voice platform that negotiates the payment amount with 
the user and, based on the outcome of the conversation, activates a workflow the 
selected plan and payment information to another system 

●  Acceptable payment options (in order): 

○  Full payment 
○  Highest amount downpayment + one more payment 
○  Settlement (up 20 % off) 
■  Max 3 payments 

○  Payment plan (no discount) 
■  Over 3 months max 
■  With biweekly, weekly or monthly payments 

●  The smallest payment can never be less than 25 % 

Requirements: 

●  The agent needs to get the payment amount provided by the user during the call and 
the negotiated amount needs to be calculated externally (outside the agent) and 
communicated back to the user. 

Objectives: 

●  Build a voice agent that handles dynamic variables  
●  Use tools to trigger functions during the call  
●  Calculate a new proposed amount in an external system and send it back to the 

agent during the call 

●  Define three outcome scenarios with different messages 
●  Based on the outcome of the call, send one of those messages to another system 
●  Bonus (optional): your agent can negotiate in multiple languages 

We will assess your solution based on these criteria: 

●  Agent performance 
●  Creativity 
●  Completeness 
●  Prompt quality 
●  Technical prowess 

Hints: 

●  Plan the conversation first, then your tool requirements 
●  You choose the external systems 
●  Prompting matters 
●  Simplicity is key 
●  Be creative
