[0:30-1:15] COMPLIANCE FEATURES
----------------------------
"First, compliance. The agent has three critical safeguards:

1. Identity Gate - The agent NEVER discloses account balance or debt
   information until the caller's identity is verified with name and
   date of birth.

2. Cease and Desist - If a caller requests no further contact in ANY
   language, the agent immediately stops negotiating, acknowledges the
   request, and ends the call professionally.

3. Floor Enforcement - The agent enforces a 25% minimum payment requirement
   and won't accept offers below that threshold."

[2:00-2:45] INTELLIGENT NEGOTIATION
----------------------------
"The agent uses a custom negotiation calculation tool to determine
counter-offers based on:
- Account balance
- Consumer's offer amount
- Attempt number
- 25% floor requirement

The tool returns different payment plan types:
- Full payment for 100% offers
- Settlement for 80%+ offers
- Split payments for 50%+ offers
- Payment plans for offers meeting the floor
- Rejection for below-floor offers"
